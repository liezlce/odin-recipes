Filipino Adobe Recipe

Adobo is a classic Filipino dish (one that I loved during my childhood and now). Its key ingredients are the chicken, soy sauce, vinegar, and peppercorns.

Ingredients

  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Peppercorns
  • Bay Leaves
  • Jalapeno
