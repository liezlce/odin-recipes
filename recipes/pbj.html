Peanut Butter and Jelly

I love PB&J.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • Peanut Butter
  • Jelly
  • (optional for me) Butter

Steps

  1. Take a knife and spread the peanut butter on one piece of bread.
  2. Use the knife and spread the jelly on the other piece of bread
  3. Put the two pieces of bread together with the spreads touching.
  4. Toddler adaptation: cut off crusts before serving.
  5. Me adaptation: Heat a pan to medium, spread butter on the top and bottom of the sandwich. Grill one side on the pan for a minute or two, flip and repeat on the otherside.
